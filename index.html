Here you can calculate your exercises

The deadlift is a basic multi-joint exercise usually performed with a barbell (as well as with a dumbbell or a weight) held with both hands.

The bench press is a basic physical exercise with free weights. The performer lies down on a bench, lowers the barbell until it touches the chest and raises it back up

The biceps barbell lift is the main exercise to increase biceps mass, which also involves the muscles of the forearm (brachial) and wrist joints

The overhead press can be performed as a basic multi-joint exercise to focus on the delts, which works the shoulder and elbow joints

Pull-ups is a basic physical exercise that develops the muscle groups of the upper body: the broad leg, biceps, brachialis, and forearms. It is performed in a hanging position.
